# Day 2: Variance, Volatility & Why They Matter

## Concept

Variance measures average squared distance from the mean. Standard deviation is the square root of variance, and in finance it is commonly called volatility.

## Market Connection

Volatility measures how unstable returns are and is used for risk management, position sizing, option pricing, and regime detection.
